The curriculum

Year 1 — Ancient. Pre-Socratics, Socrates, Plato, Aristotle, Stoicism, Epicureanism, Skepticism, Neoplatonism.

Year 2 — Medieval & Early Modern. Augustine, Islamic and Jewish philosophy, Aquinas, Machiavelli, Hobbes, Descartes, Spinoza, Leibniz, Locke, Berkeley, Hume.

Year 3 — Modern. Kant, German Idealism, Bentham, Mill, Marx, Schopenhauer, Kierkegaard, Nietzsche, American Pragmatism.

Year 4 — Contemporary. Frege, Russell, Wittgenstein, the Vienna Circle, Quine, Husserl, Heidegger, Sartre, Beauvoir, Camus, the Frankfurt School, Foucault, Derrida, Rawls, Kuhn, philosophy of mind, language, science, and ethics.
